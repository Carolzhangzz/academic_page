Research Interests

I would be always welcome to work together!
If you are interested in working in a similar area, please schedule a 30-minute conversation with me.

In my past two years of research experience, I have explored a wide range of directions, including:

  • Action Recognition: how to recognize and classify human actions in videos or images using deep learning techniques.
  • Self-Supervised Learning: how to learn from unlabeled data by designing appropriate self-supervised tasks and representations.
  • Multimodal Perception: how to effectively incorporate multiple modalities (e.g., vision, audio, language) into AI systems to make them extract more information from the surrounding world.
  • Deep Generative Models: how to use deep neural networks to model and generate data, such as images, sequences, and human motion.
